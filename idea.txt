My idea of a app is one that helps you organize the weeakly workout routines for the gym.
In a simple way, easy to understand and easy to update.
KEY FEATURES
catalog of exercises with name and image, option of share routines within users, also be able to track the progress and option to take notes. 
a) What problem does my app solve or what purpose does it serve ?.
Its gonna help the users by making their workouts and the information about progress much more easy to save, read, understand and share between friends or community.
b) Who is the intended user?
Its gonna be more directed to the people who workout in the gym and coaches.
c)What actions will users be able to perform?
sing up, select from a catalog of exercises and add their own excercises if they wish, share their routines, take notes and saving them.
